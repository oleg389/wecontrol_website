Case Studies

Moresise

In 2015, the Ukrainian market was represented only by internet banking and mobile applications from the largest banks. The National Bank of Ukraine had just begun to implement legislation regulating e-wallets. The FinTech project Simple was launched in the USA, and Rocketbank and TCS in Russia. These were the first mobile only banks, which had no analogues in Ukraine due to imperfect legislation. The lack of products for conveniently making online payments and managing personal finances was a problem that needed to be solved.

In 2015, the WeControl team members created the multibanking electronic wallet “Moresise”, the first application in Ukraine that allowed customers to:

  • connect all their bank cards in one e-wallet
  • track all their account balances
  • make money transfers (both domestic and international)
  • make payments for 3000+ services and utility bills
  • use smart PFM
